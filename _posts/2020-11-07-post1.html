As we all know WhatsApp is an instant messanger that we use on daily basis to communicate among our friends, family and even for business purposes either via text, voice or Videocall. On Nov 5th 2020, NPCI has given approval to WhatsApp Pay to operate its UPI Payments service in India. As per news reports, WhatsApp pay is now available upto maximum of only 1 lakh registered users. However, NPCI has permitted WhatsApp Pay to offer its services to 2 crores registered users initially and is expected to expand in graded manner.

WhatsApp is similiar to other UPI Payments apps such as GPay, PhonePe and Amazon Pay while BHIM (Bharat Interface for Money) is an app develeped by NPCI.

How to register for WhatsApp pay?

All you need to use WhatsApp pay is WhatsApp account registered with Indian mobile number, Bank account with same mobile number registered with bank, debit card. Make sure that you are using latest version of WhatApp, if not you must update the WhatsApp app to get latest payment service.

  1. Open WhatsApp and click on the three dots menu at the top right corner
  2. Now, click on option ‘Payments’
  3. Then, click on ‘Add payment method’



  4. Now, select your bank in the pick list
  5. WhatsApp will verify your mobile number and automatically shows your bank account registered with this mobile number
  6. Select your bank account then, click on ‘Done’.



  7. Now your setup is complete and you will now be able to see your UPI ID, your payment history and your linked bank accounts.

Remember, you can add multiple bank account to Whatsapp pay provided that your mobile number is registered with bank. Now you are ready to send money to your contacts.

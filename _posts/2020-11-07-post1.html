I will share some of my favorite destination 💗 in kerala that I visited in past two years of stay in kerala

  • Gavi

    • Gavi is one of the must-go places in kerala. It is located in the Panthanamitta district under the Ranni Forest Division. It is one of the eco-tourism places in Kerala. To visit gavi, one must get prior permission from forest deportmant. One can also apply through online via https://gavikakkionline.com. Two-wheelers are not permitted. You can get to see the view of Pamba river flowing out from Sabarimala range in Ranni Divsion

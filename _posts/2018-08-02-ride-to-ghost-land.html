Kashmir to Kanyakumari better known as #K2K among bike tourer is still a dream for most of us. Even though professional tourer like Mumbiker Nikhil and Dominar 400 Odyssey rider has set a new dimension in bike adventure in India but for amateur bike tourer it is still a dream and exploring India in their way. And this article is one such short trip to border of Srilanka.


Just a couple of people who rode about 950+ km in the state of Tamil Nadu on Old Cast Iron Bullet 350cc. We, Myself Purushothaman and my companion Poovizhi Raja took a bus from Bangalore to Salem. Actually, we planned to ride with 2 bike but end-up with poovizhi's 1973 model Bullet 350 empowered with front disc, self-start and up-straight handle bar. Finally, we started our journey from Salem to ghost town - Dhanushkodi, which borders with Srilanka diveded by Palk Strait.


Be Unstoppable like the Ocean waves.
